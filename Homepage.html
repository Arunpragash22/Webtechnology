About us

Nestled within the esteemed the AP Restaurant beckons you to embark on an unforgettable culinary journey. Our distinguished establishment boasts an expansive menu featuring an exquisite array of tantalizing dishes and delightful indulgences. From signature concoctions to classic cocktails and refreshing mocktails, our bar presents a diverse range to suit every palate. Allow yourself to be enveloped by the tranquil ambiance of the hotel as you bask in the exceptional service of our skilled bartenders, ensuring each moment spent with us is one of pure enjoyment and relaxation.
